About Me

I'm a long-time technical writer, specializing in end-user documentation. I have written, laid out, and published online and print documentation for the ordinary user and for administrators. I have worked with companies specializing in network administration, banking, healthcare, and more.

I'm now an aspiring full-stack developer, enrolled in the Coding Bootcamp at the University of Texas at Austin.

I bring a wealth of experience in creating and shipping actual products to customers. I know how to listen to the needs of internal and external customers and I'm learning how to fulfill those needs.

I'm a life-long Texan, and a proud graduate of the University of Texas at Austin.
